Instructions :

The Matplotlib Graphing App is very easy to use!

Using the Navigation bar, Users can easily zoom in, zoom out, go back and forth between postions saved in history - or return to the center. 

The graph in Red is the High Tempertatures graph, it is the first graph shown. 

The graph in Blue is the Low Tempertatures graph, to find it, press the Next or Previous button.   

Users can switch between graphs with the Previous and Next buttons.

To Exit press the top Red X button at the top corner right.
